What is Google?

Google is an American Technology Company that owns a lot Internet-realted serives. They are most famous for creating a search engine called "Google" and for owning lots of other companies like YouTube. Google is owned by a parent company called "Alphabet". Google has a total of 132,121 employees as of 2020. Google was created on September 4, 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin. The parent company of Google is worth over $1 trillion and is the thrid largest tech company in the United States. Google recives 5.6 billion searches per day which means they recive 2 trillion searches per year!

What is YouTube?

YouTube is an online video sharing platform. YouTube was created on February 14, 2005 by Jawed Karim, Chad Hurley, and Steve Chen. They created YouTube in a garage and was bought by Google in October of 2006 for $1.65 billion. Many people has gotten famous for posting videos in YouTube, the most notiable are Pewdiepie and Mrbeast. Over 1 billion hours are watched on YouTube per day. YouTube is the second most popular website just behind Google. Youtube has a wopping $15 billion annual revenue.
